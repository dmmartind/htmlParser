Abrasives

Abrasives are used to shape or finish a workpiece through rubbing and friction--leading to part of the workpiece being worn away. This process either polishes a workpiece to gain a smooth reflective surface, or roughens a workpiece for a satin, matte or beaded finish.
  • The desired finish, hardness of the work piece, and application should determined the type of abrasive used.
  • Quality abrasives should not only provide the desired finish but last longer and improve productivity.
